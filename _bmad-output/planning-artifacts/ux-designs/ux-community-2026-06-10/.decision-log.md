- **2026-06-10 · D-A11Y** — **WCAG 2.1 AA floor + low-bandwidth tolerance** (defer non-critical assets, prefer system fonts in loading states, ≤ 100KB cold-start JS budget on web). Bangla screen-reader not in v1 floor (no Bangla content).
